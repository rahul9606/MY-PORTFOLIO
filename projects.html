Project 1: SpyWare Application

Spyware is a malicious software application designed to infiltrate your computer or mobile device discreetly, without your knowledge or consent. Unlike legitimate software, spyware operates covertly, gathering sensitive information about your online activities, personal data, and more. This digital menace can compromise your privacy, security, and even the integrity of your device.

Project 3: Snake Game

The Snake Game Project is a nostalgic and entertaining programming endeavor that brings the classic Snake game into the digital realm. This project allows aspiring game developers, coders, and enthusiasts to recreate the iconic Snake game, where players control a snake to eat food and grow longer while avoiding collisions with walls and their own tail.
